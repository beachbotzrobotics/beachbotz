Our Mission

Innovation begins with a strong foundation.

At BeachBotz, we are committed to putting God first in all we do — approaching engineering, teamwork, and competition with integrity, humility, and purpose.

Through the FIRST Tech Challenge, we are training up the next generation of leaders in technology — students who are not only skilled in robotics and problem-solving, but grounded in faith, character, and service.

Our goal is to equip young people to boldly step into the future, using their gifts to make a meaningful impact in their communities and beyond.

About FIRST Tech Challenge

More than robotics.

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an international robotics program empowering students in grades 7–12 to design, build, and compete with fully functional robots. Teams collaborate to solve real-world engineering challenges while developing critical life and career skills — preparing the next generation of innovators, engineers, and leaders.
